Our Story

Where Rwandan
hills meet
a Polish café

"Coffee is not a drink — it is a conversation between the earth and a person."

Ganzi was born from a simple belief: that the world's finest coffee should reach cups directly, without middlemen. We work directly with a farmers' cooperative in Rwanda's Southern Province, where volcanic soils and a tropical climate create ideal conditions for arabica with an exceptional flavour profile.

Poland has long cherished slow-brewed coffee — sipped in good company, without hurry. Ganzi continues that café tradition, offering specialty coffee that unites the warmth of Polish hospitality with the wild nature of Rwanda. Every batch is roasted in a small roastery on the outskirts of Kraków, with deep respect for the bean and the craft.
